Bonaberi-Douala

Bonaberi is a district in Douala, Cameroon's largest city and economic capital, located on the Wouri River's right bank. It's also known as Douala IV and has a surface area of 890 square kilometers. Bonaberi is home to many industries, markets, and transportation facilities, including the Bonaberi-Douala industrial estate, which has direct access to the Bonaberi port and indirect access to Douala port. The industrial estate is home to a wide range of industries, including cement, and has well-established infrastructure. Bonaberi is also a major source of water for many households in informal settlements.
