Intermediate Adventure

Roll-Up We Going Deep

Dive into a full-day expedition along the River Rapids, where the echoes of the Gold Rush era still resonate in the rushing waters and rugged terrain. This intermediate-level journey promises a thrilling blend of challenging Class II and III rapids interspersed with tranquil stretches that allow you to soak in the stunning natural beauty. Place History: The River Rapids were once a lifeline for pioneers during the Gold Rush, who navigated its waters in search of precious minerals. Evidence of their presence can still be found in abandoned mining camps and relics along the riverbanks, offering a glimpse into the region's adventurous past.

Social Facts: The nearby town is a hub of artistic expression, with galleries and workshops showcasing works inspired by the river's wild landscapes. Artists from across the region gather here, drawn by the majestic views and the community's rich cultural tapestry. Visitors can explore local art studios, participate in workshops, and even purchase one-of-a-kind pieces as souvenirs of their rafting adventure.

Food: Lunchtime brings a riverside barbecue, featuring hearty fare like grilled venison, cornbread, and fresh salads made from locally sourced ingredients. The meal not only fuels your adventure but also provides a taste of the region's robust culinary traditions, passed down through generations of river dwellers and outdoor enthusiasts.

Cool Stuff: Discover hidden hot springs along the riverbanks, where natural thermal pools offer a soothing retreat after a day of exhilarating rafting. These geothermal wonders are treasured by locals and visitors alike, providing a unique opportunity to relax and rejuvenate amidst nature's splendor.
