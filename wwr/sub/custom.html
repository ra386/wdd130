Custom Expedition

See Beyond The Sky

Tailor your adventure along the River Custom, where diverse ecosystems converge to create a tapestry of natural wonders and ecological diversity. This customizable experience allows you to explore specific interests, whether it's birdwatching, botany, or conservation efforts that pique your curiosity. Place History: The River Custom area is steeped in natural history, showcasing the evolution of diverse ecosystems and their ecological significance. Learn about ongoing conservation efforts and the delicate balance needed to preserve the river's pristine habitats for future generations.

Social Facts: Local conservationists lead river clean-up initiatives, inviting visitors to participate in efforts to protect the habitats of endangered species and ensure the river remains a haven for wildlife. These initiatives reflect a commitment to sustainability and environmental stewardship, ensuring that every rafting trip contributes to the ongoing preservation of this natural treasure.

Food: Gourmet picnics feature organic produce sourced from nearby farms and artisanal treats that showcase the region's culinary diversity. Sample farm-fresh cheeses, wildflower honey, and freshly baked breads as you dine amidst breathtaking scenery, savoring flavors that reflect the river's bounty and the community's commitment to sustainable agriculture.

Cool Stuff: Witness the annual salmon run, a natural spectacle that draws visitors and wildlife enthusiasts from around the world. Watch as these majestic fish navigate the river's currents to spawn, an awe-inspiring display of nature's resilience and the interconnectedness of river ecosystems.
